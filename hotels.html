Aruba Surf Club:

Experience the vibrant Caribbean spirit at Marriott's Aruba Surf Club in Palm Beach, where you can enjoy a variety of amenities including water sports, a lazy river, tennis, and a kids' club. Our oceanfront resort offers spacious, tropical-themed villas with ocean or garden views, full kitchens, and comfortable living areas, as well as convenient guest rooms with kitchenettes. With on-site dining, complimentary WiFi, and no resort fees, a memorable family vacation awaits.
